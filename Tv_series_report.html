🏁 Overview

Formula 1: Drive to Survive is a British documentary series produced by Netflix in collaboration with Formula One. The series provides a behind-the-scenes look into the world of Formula 1 racing, including the lives of drivers, the dynamics within teams, and the drama of the racing season.

First released in 2019, the show has aired seven seasons as of March 7, 2025.

It has played a significant role in boosting global interest in Formula 1.

A crossover event with Full Swing broadcast on Netflix in November 2023 called The Netflix Cup.

plt.title('Viewership per Season')
plt.xlabel('Season')
plt.ylabel('Viewership (millions)')
plt.grid(True)
plt.show()

import numpy as np

changes = np.diff(viewers)

plt.bar(seasons[1:], changes, color='blue')
plt.title('Season-to-Season Change in Viewership')
plt.xlabel('Season')
plt.ylabel('Change in Viewership (millions)')
plt.axhline(0, color='black', linewidth=0.8)
plt.show()

🧾 Observations

  • The largest increase in viewership occurred between Seasons 2 and 3.
  • After this peak, the growth in viewership from season to season has slowed down.
  • This suggests that the series may have reached its prime audience, and future growth in viewership could be limited.
